# SongBrief Design Notes

- Keep the app data-first and quiet: rankings, totals, and scan state should be visible without marketing copy.
- Use restrained glass surfaces with subtle borders and shadows. Avoid heavy blur, decorative blobs, or dense gradients.
- Preserve the current interaction model: refresh, ranking tabs, and pull-to-refresh should remain functional.
- Prefer readable comparison patterns for stats, especially horizontal proportional bars for rankings.
